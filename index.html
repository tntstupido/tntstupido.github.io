ABOUT

I'm Mladen Stojanović, a game developer focused on mobile-first action and survival experiences. I care most about what players feel moment-to-moment: movement response, hit feedback, readable chaos, and progression that keeps each run fresh.

Alongside production work, I prototype VR mechanics to test interaction ideas and bring the best parts back into mobile design where performance and clarity matter most.

DESIGN FOCUS

My workflow is built around practical game pillars that match modern mobile play patterns.

Session Design

Progression Systems
