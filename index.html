ABOUT

I'm Mladen Stojanović, a mobile developer building both games and productivity apps. On the games side, I care most about what players feel moment-to-moment: movement response, hit feedback, readable chaos, and progression that keeps each run fresh. On the apps side, I build tools like Roomli that help people manage shared households — shopping, chores, bills, and everyday tasks — with the same mobile-first clarity.

Alongside production work, I prototype VR mechanics to test interaction ideas and bring the best parts back into mobile design where performance and simplicity matter most.

GAME DESIGN FOCUS

My game workflow is built around practical pillars that match modern mobile play patterns.

Session Design

Short, high-intensity runs that fit real mobile usage patterns: quick start, clear stakes, strong payoff.

Progression Systems

In-run upgrades plus meta progression to create meaningful build choices and long-term retention.

Tone and Atmosphere

Dark worlds with sarcastic personality, hand-crafted silhouettes, and readable combat under pressure.
