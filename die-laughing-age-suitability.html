Age Suitability for Die Laughing

App Store calculated age rating: 13+

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

This page provides additional age-suitability context for Die Laughing to help players, parents, and guardians understand the app's content profile.

1. Audience

Die Laughing is intended for teens and adults who enjoy stylized roguelite action gameplay and dark-comedy fantasy themes.

2. Content Summary

Horror and Tone

  • Frequent horror/fear themes (graveyard atmosphere, monsters, undead motifs).
  • Sarcastic/dark-comedy presentation style.

Violence and Weapons

  • Frequent fantasy/cartoon combat against enemy creatures.
  • Frequent use of non-realistic weapons and attack effects.
  • No prolonged graphic or sadistic realistic violence.

Sexual Content and Nudity

  • None.

Alcohol, Tobacco, and Drugs

  • None.

Profanity / Crude Language

  • No strong profanity.

3. Interactive and Online Features

  • In-app purchases may be available.
  • Advertising may be shown, depending on build/platform.
  • No real-money gambling.
  • No loot boxes that sell randomized virtual items.
  • No in-app user chat or user-generated content sharing.
  • No unrestricted in-app web browsing.

4. Parent/Guardian Notes

  • If your child is sensitive to horror themes, supervise play before regular use.
  • You can use device-level parental controls and in-app purchase restrictions provided by your platform account settings.
