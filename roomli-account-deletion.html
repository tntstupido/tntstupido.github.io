Roomli Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Use this page to permanently delete your Roomli account and associated household data, using the same credentials created in the app.

Delete Your Account

What Gets Deleted

  • Your Roomli account identity.
  • Household memberships owned by your account.
  • Household-linked data managed by Roomli backend (tasks, shopping, bills, expenses, invites), according to backend delete rules.

What May Be Retained

  • Minimal logs retained for legal/security obligations where required.
  • App store billing records managed by Google/Apple (not controlled by Roomli backend).
