WHAT IS SLASHER MANAGER?

Slasher Manager turns the slasher horror subgenre into a satisfying incremental game. You start with a single victim click and grow into a mass-casualty operation across 9 summer-camp-themed levels.

It is designed as a low-pressure idle game with a darkly comic tone — every upgrade and achievement leans into the absurdity of managing a horror franchise like a business.

FEATURES

Click, upgrade, repeat — with depth for the long haul.

Satisfying Click Loop

One-tap kills, passive auto-progression, and big-number moments as your body count climbs.

9 Themed Levels

Each level brings new victims, settings, and difficulty modifiers — from summer camp to suburbia to hospital.

60+ Upgrades

Build your arsenal from classics to improvised methods, each with its own stat curve and visual.

Prestige Skill Tree

Reset for permanent bonuses across 4 branches — Combat, Economy, Meta, and Risk.

50+ Achievements

Meta goals to chase between runs, from progression milestones to mastery and secret unlocks.

Hand-Drawn Notebook

Sketch aesthetic with torn paper, ink textures, masking tape, and typewriter fonts.
