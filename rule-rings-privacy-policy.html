Privacy Policy for Rule Rings

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Developer: Mladen Stojanovic

Rule Rings ("the Game", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected when you use the Game, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require your real name, phone number, or physical address to play Rule Rings.

Rule Rings uses Firebase Analytics. This service may collect technical and usage information such as:

  • Device and app information (for example device model, OS version, app version, and language)
  • Approximate region and IP-derived technical signals
  • In-app usage events and session activity
  • Diagnostic and performance-related analytics signals

2. How We Use Data

We use analytics data to:

  • Operate and maintain the Game
  • Understand gameplay usage and improve balance/features
  • Monitor app stability and performance
  • Detect abuse and prevent technical issues

3. Third-Party Service

Rule Rings currently uses:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell your personal information for money.

We only share data with service providers as needed to provide analytics and improve the Game.

5. Data Retention

  • Gameplay progress stored locally remains on your device until removed by uninstalling the app or clearing app data.
  • Analytics data retention is controlled by Firebase/Google settings and policies.
  • Support emails are retained only as needed to respond to your request.

6. Your Choices

  • You can uninstall the Game to stop future in-app data collection.
  • You can manage relevant privacy and advertising settings in your device and platform account.
  • You can contact us with privacy requests at mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Rule Rings is not directed to children under 13 (or the higher minimum age required by local law). If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to address the issue.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own by our service providers, subject to applicable law.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal data. Contact us at mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com to submit a request.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
