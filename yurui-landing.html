Gentle Habit

WHAT IS YURUI?

Yurui (ゆるい) means gentle. The app is designed for people who want to build habits without anxiety from broken streaks or all-or-nothing pressure.

It focuses on one active habit at a time and uses a calm "never miss twice" mindset. You track progress with simple check-ins and completion rate, then keep moving.

FEATURES

Built to keep momentum gentle and sustainable.

One Habit at a Time

Stay focused on a single active habit to reduce overwhelm and improve consistency.

No Streak Guilt

Progress is measured through completion rate and consistency, not fragile streak chains.

Gentle Reminders

Optional reminders help you show up without pressure-heavy notification patterns.

Share Cards

Share your calm progress with polished cards when you want external accountability.

Theme Personalization

Premium themes let you adjust the visual mood to keep the app feeling pleasant every day.

Offline First

Core habit data is stored locally for fast daily check-ins and reliable tracking.
