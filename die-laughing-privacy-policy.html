Privacy Policy for Die Laughing

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

Developer: Mladen Stojanovic
Contact: mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com

Die Laughing ("the Game", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data may be collected when you use the Game, how it is used, and your choices.

Summary

  • The Game works offline. No internet connection is required to play.
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, home address, or government identifier.
  • We do not collect location data — neither precise (GPS) nor coarse (IP-based, network-based, or cell-based).
  • We do not read your contacts, calendar, photos, microphone, camera, SMS, call logs, or health data.
  • The only identifiers we process are: an anonymous analytics identifier (Firebase), advertising identifiers (AdMob, AppsFlyer, Meta when ads/attribution are enabled), and a hashed session identifier.
  • You can request deletion of any data tied to you at any time by contacting us.

1. Information We Collect

Short version: we do not collect your name, email, phone number, home address, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, location, or any other personal identifier. The Game can be played offline. When online features are enabled, only the data described below is processed, and only by the third-party providers listed in Section 3.

Depending on platform, build, and features you use, the following categories of data may be processed:

a) Device and App Data

  • Device model, OS version, app version, and language
  • Anonymous technical identifiers used for analytics, anti-fraud, ad delivery, or measurement (where enabled)

We do not collect precise or coarse location data, and we do not read your IP address to determine your location for our own purposes. If a third-party ad provider infers a coarse region from your IP for ad serving, that processing happens entirely on their side under their own privacy policy (see Section 3) and is not used by us.

b) In-App Purchase Data

If you make purchases, the platform provider (for example Apple or Google) processes payment data. We may receive purchase-related records such as:

  • Transaction status and purchase token/receipt metadata
  • Product identifier (item bought)
  • Purchase timestamp and validation status

We do not receive your full card number, bank details, billing address, or any payment credentials.

e) Install Attribution (If Enabled)

When attribution providers are enabled, they may process data needed to measure whether an install came from their advertising campaigns, including:

  • Advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID, Apple IDFA where available)
  • Install referrer information (Google Play install referrer)
  • Attribution source, campaign, and click identifiers

This data is used solely for measuring install sources and is not used by us for cross-app tracking-based advertising.

f) Ads and Ad Measurement (If Enabled)

If ads are enabled, ad providers may process ad-related data including:

  • Advertising identifier (for example the Android Advertising ID or Apple's IDFA) and app set identifier
  • Ad impressions, clicks, views, and diagnostics
  • Basic device/app signals for fraud prevention and ad performance

Where required by law, consent prompts may be shown before tracking-based ads are used. The advertising identifier is the only user-resettable identifier shared for ad purposes, and you can reset or limit it at any time from your device settings.

g) Gameplay, Analytics, and Diagnostics

  • Gameplay progress and settings (for example wave reached, weapons used, run duration)
  • Crash logs, stack traces, and performance diagnostics
  • Anonymous usage events needed to maintain, debug, balance, and improve the Game

These events are tied to an anonymous analytics identifier, not to your real-world identity.

h) Optional Account-Linked Features

If account-linked features are enabled (for example cloud save or leaderboard integrations), provider-specific identifiers and feature activity may be processed by those providers.

  • Platform identity identifiers (for example Google Play Games / Game Center player identifiers)
  • Backend user identifiers (for example Supabase user_id) used to maintain your profile/progression and leaderboard records
  • Display name/nickname used for leaderboard display
  • Cloud progression/profile records associated with your gameplay identity

These account-linked user identifiers are used for app functionality and analytics related to game operation, and are not used by us for cross-app tracking-based advertising. If you choose not to use account-linked features, none of this data is collected.

i) Support Communications

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you include, such as your email address and message content. This is used only to respond to your request.

14. Website Analytics (die-laughing-landing.html and related pages)

The Die Laughing landing page uses Umami Analytics (https://umami.is/policy) to measure traffic and link clicks. Umami is cookieless, does not collect personal data, does not use persistent identifiers, and does not require a consent banner. The only data recorded is:

  • The page URL visited (for example /die-laughing-landing.html)
  • The referrer source (where the visitor came from)
  • The visitor's country (derived from the IP at request time, not stored)
  • The visitor's browser and operating system
  • Custom click events on the App Store and Google Play buttons, with the platform (iOS / Android) and the page section (hero, final_cta, mobile_sticky) where the click happened

No IP addresses are stored, no device fingerprinting, and no cross-site tracking. The same privacy guarantees apply to the rest of the portfolio site at mladenstojanovic.art, which uses the same Umami setup.
