Privacy Policy for Die Laughing

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

Developer: Mladen Stojanovic
Contact: mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com

Die Laughing ("the Game", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data may be collected when you use the Game, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require your real name, phone number, or home address to play the Game.

Depending on platform, build, and features you use, the following categories of data may be processed:

a) Device and App Data

  • Device model, OS version, app version, and language
  • IP address and approximate region inferred from IP
  • Technical identifiers used for analytics, anti-fraud, ad delivery, or measurement (where enabled)

b) In-App Purchase Data

If you make purchases, the platform provider (for example Apple or Google) processes payment data. We may receive purchase-related records such as:

  • Transaction status and purchase token/receipt metadata
  • Product identifier (item bought)
  • Purchase timestamp and validation status

We do not receive your full card number or bank details.

c) Ads and Ad Measurement (If Enabled)

If ads are enabled, ad providers may process ad-related data including:

  • Advertising identifier and app set identifier
  • Ad impressions, clicks, views, and diagnostics
  • Basic device/app signals for fraud prevention and ad performance

Where required by law, consent prompts may be shown before tracking-based ads are used.

d) Gameplay, Analytics, and Diagnostics

  • Gameplay progress and settings
  • Crash logs, stack traces, and performance diagnostics
  • Usage events needed to maintain, debug, and improve the Game

e) Optional Account-Linked Features

If account-linked features are enabled (for example cloud save or leaderboard integrations), provider-specific identifiers and feature activity may be processed by those providers.

  • Platform identity identifiers (for example Google Play Games / Game Center player identifiers)
  • Backend user identifiers (for example Supabase user_id) used to maintain your profile/progression and leaderboard records
  • Display name/nickname used for leaderboard display
  • Cloud progression/profile records associated with your gameplay identity

These account-linked user identifiers are used for app functionality and analytics related to game operation, and are not used by us for cross-app tracking-based advertising.

f) Support Communications

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you include, such as your email address and message content.

2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Provide and operate the Game
  • Process and validate purchases
  • Deliver and measure ads (if enabled)
  • Sync progress where cloud features are enabled
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Improve stability, balance, and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Applicable providers depend on platform/build and enabled features:

  • Apple (App Store / In-App Purchases): https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • Google (AdMob, Play Services, Billing, Play Games where used): https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase (Analytics / Crash diagnostics where used): https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Supabase (cloud profile/leaderboard backend where used): https://supabase.com/privacy

These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local progress data remains on your device until removed by uninstalling the app or clearing app data.
  • Cloud/account-linked data retention is managed by the relevant provider under its policies.
  • Support emails are retained as needed to resolve requests and maintain records.
  • Third-party retention periods are controlled by those providers.

You can request deletion of data we directly control by contacting: mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com.

For account/data deletion instructions and request format, use: Die Laughing Account & Data Deletion.

5. Your Choices and Controls

  • Manage app permissions in device settings
  • Reset or limit ad personalization in platform settings
  • Allow or deny tracking permission prompts where applicable
  • Uninstall the Game to stop future in-app collection
  • Contact us for privacy requests

6. Sale and Sharing

We do not sell your personal information for money.

We may share data with service providers only as needed to operate the Game, process purchases, deliver ads (if enabled), detect fraud, and provide analytics/crash diagnostics.

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13 (or the higher minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to address the issue.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own by us or our providers, subject to applicable law.

10. Account and Data Deletion

Die Laughing does not provide username/password account creation directly in-app. Identity-linked features can use platform identity providers (for example Google Play Games on Android and Game Center on iOS).

You can request deletion of backend account-linked gameplay data through our deletion page:

https://mladenstojanovic.art/die-laughing-account-deletion.html

When a valid request is confirmed, we delete data we directly control (for example backend profile/progression and linked leaderboard identity rows) unless retention is required by law or security obligations.

Platform account records held by Google/Apple are controlled by those providers under their own policies.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain personal data, and to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us at mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and change the "Last Updated" date.

13. Contact

14. Platform Notes

Some services described above may be available only on specific platforms or in specific releases. For example, Android-only integrations may not be active in iOS builds, and vice versa. This policy covers Die Laughing releases and related support channels across supported platforms.
