Privacy Policy for Die Laughing

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Developer: Mladen Stojanovic
Contact: mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com

Die Laughing ("the Game", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected when you use the Game, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask for your name, phone number, or home address to play the Game.

Depending on features you use, the following data may be processed:

a) Advertising and Device Data (AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK)

If ads are shown, third-party ad providers (such as Google AdMob) may collect or receive data including:

  • Advertising ID (Ad_ID)
  • App set ID
  • Device and app information (device model, OS version, app version, language)
  • IP address and approximate location (derived from IP)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, views)
  • Diagnostics and fraud-prevention signals

b) In-App Purchase Data (Google Play Billing)

If you make purchases, Google processes payment data.
We may receive purchase-related information such as:

  • Purchase token / transaction status
  • Product identifier (item bought)
  • Purchase timestamp and validation status

We do not receive your full card number or bank details.

c) Google Play Games Services (Achievements, Leaderboards, Cloud Save)

If enabled, Google Play Games Services may process:

  • Player ID and profile-related identifiers
  • Achievements and leaderboard activity
  • Cloud Save metadata and saved game data synchronized through Google services

Cloud Save data is used to synchronize your progress across supported devices/accounts.

d) Gameplay and Diagnostic Data

We may process:

  • Local gameplay progress and settings
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Basic usage events needed to maintain and improve the Game

e) Support Communications

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you include (for example, your email address and message content).

2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Provide and operate the Game
  • Synchronize game progress across devices (Cloud Save, where enabled)
  • Deliver and measure ads
  • Process and validate purchases
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Improve stability, balance, and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

The Game may use third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Ads): https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases): https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Games Services (Achievements, Leaderboards, Cloud Save): https://policies.google.com/privacy

These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above:

  • Local save/progress data stays on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.
  • Cloud Save data (if enabled) is stored and managed through Google Play Games Services under Google's retention policies.
  • Support emails are retained as needed to resolve requests and for reasonable record-keeping.
  • Third-party providers (for example Google) retain data according to their own policies.

You can request deletion of data we directly control by contacting: mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com.
For Play Games Cloud Save data, deletion/management may also be available through your Google account and Google Play Games settings.

5. Your Choices and Controls

You can:

  • Reset or limit ad personalization in your Android device settings
  • Manage app permissions in device settings
  • Uninstall the Game to stop further data collection by the app
  • Contact us for privacy-related requests

For users in regions requiring consent (such as EEA/UK), consent options for advertising may be shown in-app where required.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country).
We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. If you believe a child provided data to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. International Data Transfers

Third-party providers may process data on servers in different countries. By using the Game, you understand that data may be transferred and processed outside your country, subject to applicable law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Last Updated" date.
