Slasher Manager Age Suitability

Slasher Manager is a horror idle/clicker game. It contains stylized horror themes. Below is a content summary to help players, parents, and platform reviewers understand what the Game includes.

Recommended for ages 16+

1. Recommended Age

Slasher Manager is recommended for players aged 16 and older. The Game contains stylized violence, horror themes, and dark humor. We do not direct the Game to children under 13 (or the higher minimum age required by local law).

2. Content Categories

The Game contains the following content. All depictions are stylized and non-photorealistic.

  • Stylized violence: Cartoon-style character icons are "eliminated" by clicking. No realistic gore or detailed injury depictions.
  • Horror themes: Slasher / summer-camp horror setting, masked characters, suspicious NPCs. The tone is darkly comic rather than grim.
  • Dark humor: Achievements, upgrade names, and UI copy lean into the absurdity of running a horror franchise. Examples include reference-only body-count displays and satirical level descriptions.
  • Blood: Red accent elements (screams counter, kill animations on icons) but no detailed wound imagery.

3. What the Game Does Not Include

  • No sexual content or nudity
  • No real-world drug or alcohol use
  • No realistic graphic violence or torture depictions
  • No multiplayer interaction with other players (single-player only)
  • No user-generated content visible to other players

4. Parental Guidance

If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about whether the Game is appropriate for a specific player, please contact us at mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com. Steam also offers family visibility controls and parental PIN options through the Steam Family View feature.

5. Rating Status

Final age rating labels (such as ESRB, PEGI, USK, ACB) will be assigned by the relevant regional rating body before public release on Steam. This page will be updated to reflect the official rating once assigned.
