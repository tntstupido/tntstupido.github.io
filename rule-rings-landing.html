Rule Rings

WHAT IS RULE RINGS?

Rule Rings is built around one core puzzle question: can you place every piece so all visible constraints are true simultaneously?

It is designed as a durable daily puzzle habit with clean visuals, readable rules, and satisfying "click" moments when the final arrangement resolves.

FEATURES

Simple to read, deep to solve.

Overlapping Ring Logic

Tokens placed in overlap zones satisfy multiple ring constraints at once, creating strong deduction chains.

Readable Rule Language

Short visual rules around color, shape, fill, and quantity keep every puzzle understandable on mobile.

Daily Puzzle Habit

Built for quick but meaningful daily sessions with steady difficulty progression.

Calm Presentation

Minimal, low-noise visual style focused on clarity rather than speed or chaos.

System-First Scalability

Rule combinations and board layouts support long-term puzzle variety without heavy content overhead.

Mobile-First Interaction

Drag-and-place token flow optimized for touch readability and satisfying solve feedback.
