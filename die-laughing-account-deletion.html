Die Laughing Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of account-linked data for Die Laughing: Survival Rogue.

1. How to Request Deletion

Send an email to: mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com

Subject line recommendation: Die Laughing - Data Deletion Request

Please include, when available:

  • Your platform and account context (Android Play Games or iOS Game Center)
  • Your in-game display name/nickname
  • Any known user identifier shown in support/debug screens (if available)
  • A short confirmation that you want backend account-linked data deleted

2. What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Backend profile/progression data we directly control
  • Backend leaderboard identity linkage and related entries we directly control
  • Support-request records, where deletion is legally permitted

3. What Is Not Deleted by Us

  • Google Play Games account data managed by Google
  • Game Center account data managed by Apple
  • Payment records managed by platform stores/payment providers
  • Data that must be retained for legal/security obligations

4. Processing Time

We aim to respond to valid requests within 30 days, subject to verification and legal requirements.
