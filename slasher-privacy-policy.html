Privacy Policy for Slasher Manager

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

Developer: Mladen Stojanovic
Contact: mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com

Slasher Manager ("the Game", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected when you use the Game, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require your real name, phone number, or physical address to play Slasher Manager.

Slasher Manager may use analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics or a self-hosted equivalent) to collect technical and usage information such as:

  • Device and app information (for example device model, OS version, app version, and language)
  • Approximate region and IP-derived technical signals
  • In-app usage events and session activity
  • Diagnostic and performance-related analytics signals

Gameplay progress (such as Screams, kills, current level, prestige count, and achievement state) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Steam Cloud if you sign in to Steam.

2. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • Operate and maintain the Game
  • Understand gameplay usage and improve balance, features, and difficulty curves
  • Monitor app stability and performance
  • Detect abuse and prevent technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

Slasher Manager currently uses or may use:

  • Steam (Valve Corporation): https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement
  • Firebase Analytics (Google): https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

These providers process data under their own privacy policies. We will update this section before launching if additional services are added.

4. Steam Cloud Saves

Slasher Manager supports Steam Cloud for save synchronization across devices. Cloud saves are stored on Steam's servers and associated with your Steam account. You can manage Cloud sync for Slasher Manager from your Steam client (Game Properties → Cloud). For more details, see the Steam Privacy Agreement.

5. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell your personal information for money.

We only share data with service providers as needed to provide analytics, cloud saves, and improve the Game.

6. Data Retention

  • Gameplay progress stored locally remains on your device until removed by uninstalling the Game or clearing app data.
  • Steam Cloud saves are retained per Steam's data retention policies.
  • Analytics data retention is controlled by the relevant service provider's policies.
  • Support emails are retained only as needed to respond to your request.

7. Your Choices

  • You can uninstall the Game to stop future in-app data collection.
  • You can disable Steam Cloud sync from the Steam client.
  • You can contact us with privacy requests at mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Slasher Manager is not directed to children under 13 (or the higher minimum age required by local law). The Game contains horror themes including stylized violence that may not be appropriate for young audiences — see our Age Suitability page for details. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to address the issue.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own by our service providers, subject to applicable law.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal data. Contact us at mladenstojanovicpo@gmail.com to submit a request. For Steam Cloud data, see our Data Deletion page.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
