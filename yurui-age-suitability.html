Age Suitability for Yurui

Designed for general audiences

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

This page provides age-suitability context for Yurui to help users, parents, and guardians understand the app's content profile.

1. Audience

Yurui is designed for teens and adults who want a calm, simple habit-building experience.

2. Content Summary

Violence and Mature Content

  • No violence or graphic content.
  • No sexual content or nudity.
  • No gambling-related content.
  • No alcohol, tobacco, or drug references.

Language and Tone

  • Calm, supportive, and non-harsh coaching language.
  • No explicit or strong profanity.

3. Interactive and Online Features

  • Optional in-app purchases for premium features.
  • No user-to-user chat or social feed in core experience.
  • No unrestricted web browsing inside the app.

4. Parent/Guardian Notes

  • Yurui can be used by younger users with normal device-level purchase controls enabled.
  • Guardians can manage in-app purchase restrictions in platform account settings.
